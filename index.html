About Satviru Caters

Traditional Taste, Modern Presentation

Satviru Caterers has been serving authentic Sri Lankan cuisine for over 15 years. We take pride in preserving traditional recipes while presenting them with contemporary elegance.

Our team of experienced chefs uses only the freshest local ingredients to create memorable dining experiences for weddings, corporate events, and private parties across Sri Lanka.
